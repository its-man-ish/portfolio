ARKA, the official astronomy and physics club of IlT(ISM), Dhanbad was founded on 5th December 2017. The main vision of our clubis to bring like-minded people, who share the fascination with space, stars, and galaxies, and provide them with a platform to discuss and finally shape their hobby into something fruitful and evolve as amateur astronomers.

We, The Students at ARKA are driven by the desire to explore and understand the working and the wonders of the cosmos, which hold a billion secrets! The club was founded on the 5th of December 2017 by six esteemed seniors who wanted to provide a platform for students who wished to seek the answers to the universe's biggest questions. Although initially students may not know much about astronomy it was the senior's desire that through working with the club we may learn more about the universe.

We joined this college to pursue technical knowledge and this club not only provides us with that knowledge but also let's us follow our interests. To help uS gain technical knowledge the club holds frequent seminars, space talks and events with prominent researchers. Our club's Dobsonian telescope provides us with the ability to view the wonders of the night sky. We also organize various competitions and keep a watch on the latest news and discoveries in the field of astronomy to keep our fellow students updated on these fascinating topics. Right noW as a club we are in our early stages.
